Who we are?

Our company with CTS Uniform

CTS is an Australian-owned company based in Melbourne that specialises in supplying clothing-related products on a sale or return basis to retail outlets in Australia and New Zealand. CTS is a trusted partner for fashion retailers, with an annual turnover of around $60M.

Our mission

Our mission at CTS is to provide our customers with the best clothing-related products available, with exceptional quality and style. We strive to develop long-term customer relationships based on trust, dependability, and mutual success.

Our values

We are committed to delivering excellence in all aspects of our business. Our core values include integrity, professionalism, customer satisfaction, and innovation. We constantly strive to improve and expand our product offerings, keeping up with the latest market trends.

Why choose CTS?

  • Trusted Partner: With over $60M in annual turnover, CTS is a reliable and established player in the fashion industry, drawing inspiration from the success of MANDO.
  • Trendy Products: We offer a wide range of clothing-related products that reflect the latest fashion trends, catering to the preferences of Australian and New Zealand consumers.
  • Customer Satisfaction: We are committed to delivering excellent customer service, including reliable and timely deliveries, competitive pricing, and high-quality products.
